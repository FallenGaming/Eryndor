In Eryndor, strength and magic are measured by a universal Ranking System recognized across all nations, guilds, and factions. Though some nations and cultures have different names for these distinct ranks. Every individual possesses two distinct ranks.

**Combat Rank** - physical power, martial skill, weapon mastery and endurance.

**Magic Rank** - sorcery, spirit force, or innate supernatural talent.

### Recognized Ranks
**Unranked** - everyday civilians without any form of training.
**D** - novices, common soldiers, apprentices
**C** - trained fighters, mages, and minor adventurers
**B** - experienced warriors, skilled mages, rising elites
**A** - heroes, warlord, arch-mages. Names known to nations.
**S** - Legendary individuals who shape history and rival armies
**SS** - Beings whose power can topple kingdoms and shatter landscapes
**SSS** - Mythic beings, divine avatars, or monsters that rival gods.

### Progression
Progression through the ranks is overseen by the Combat Guilds and Mage Orders, who provide trials, recognition, and certification. Ranks are more than titles, they open doors to contracts, resources, and authority across all realms.

Higher ranks do not merely show the difference in power between individuals. They reshape perception, aura, and destiny. An S-rank warrior radiates a pressence that can unnerve soldiers, an SS mage's spells warp reality around them, and SSS ranked beings may bend the laws of the world.
