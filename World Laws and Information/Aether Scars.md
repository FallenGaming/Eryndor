[[Aether]] scars are open wounds of the world, places where the natural cycle of [[Aether]] has been ruptured beyond recovery.
They form when [[Aether]] is drawn faster then it can be replenished, leaving the world hollow, unstable, and hungry. The very fabric of reality fractures in these zones, light bends wrong, air shimmers like oil, and time itself seems to stutter.

# Formation
Most scars begin as [[Aether Wells]] that have been drained dry by reckless spellcraft or ancient calamities, Others were born from [[Corruption]] Outbreaks, where the fusion of blood and [[Aether]] corrupts life itself. Once the flow is disrupted, the leyline beneath collapses, creating a wound that seeps raw chaotic energy into the surroundings.

# Environmental Effects
* Flora blackens or crystalizes
* Fauna mutate into monstrous forms
* Terrain: Stone becomes brittle or glassy, rivers boil, and ash storms rise without warning
* Weather: Local climates collapse. It may rain fire, freeze without warning, create hurricane winds, rip tears into the fabric of the world. 
* Perceptions: Travelers report hearing whispers or seeing impossible reflections, the dying memories of the land itself.

# Inhabitants and Dangers
The Scars attract abominations born of [[corruption]]. These creatures feed on magic and life-force alike, making them extremely dangerous, especially to mages.

The [[Abyssal Brood]] thrive in these zones, drawing power from the chaos and spreading the taint outwards. Demon cults, rogue sorcerers, and even [[Aether]] miners risk venturing into the Scars to harvest volatile [[Aether]] Crystals, though few return.

# Purification and Containment
Restoring balance to a Scar is nearly impossible. The [[Azur Enclave]] and [[Greenwood Pact|Greenwood]] druids attempt containment through ritual warding and ley realignment, while the [[silver crown]] favors sanctified bombardment - complete annihilation of the area. 

Only a few legendary [[Aether Sanctums|Sanctums]] are known to have healed from Scars, usually through divine or world-altering intervention.
# Notable Aether Scars
* The Hollow Spire: a spiraling canyon of molten glass said to hum with the screams of trapped souls.
* Blackfall Basin: a crater lake that glows violet at night and drains the life of any that approach.
* Ashmere Vale: once fertile farmland, now a barren plain where crops sprout into crystal and shatter at dawn.
