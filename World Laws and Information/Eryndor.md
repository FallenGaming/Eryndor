Eryndor exists as a single material plane, an immense and varied continent surrounded by the [[Endless Sea]], a boundless expanse few have crossed and none have mapped. All life shares this single stretch of land. The great races of [[Humans|Man]], [[Elves|Elf]], and [[Dwarves|Dwarf]]; the beast-kin tribes and demonic courts; and the horrors born of [[Corruption]] that fester in shadowed places.

The continent is fractured by mountain ranges, rivers, and political boundaries that mirror the differences between its people. Kingdoms rise and fall not from divine decree but from ambitions, greed, and faith. It is a land of empires and ruins, of ancient secrets beneath cities, where the bones of the gods lie beneath the soil.


# Shape of the Continent

Eryndor's landmass is roughly oval, bisected by the [[Shardspine Range]], a colossal mountain chain that runs north to south, separating east and west.

To the north the mountains run west, consuming most of the northern tip. The [[Ember Forgeholds]] lay claim to this region. 

Directly south of the forgeholds, the [[Silver Crown]]'s borders stretch nearly the entire western half of the continent.

The [[Greenwood Pact]] rests just beneath the [[Silver Crown]], acting as a border between them and the [[Abyssal Brood]].

The [[Abyssal Brood]] consumes the southern section of Eryndor, their feast stretching from east to west.

Directly north of the Abyssal Broods eastern tip lies the [[Crimson Court]].

Spanning most of the eastern half, the [[Gynareth Dominion]] claims nearly as much as the [[Silver Crown]].

Directly above Gynareth, the [[Azur Enclave]] occupies the north eastern tip.

# Scale and Geography
Eryndor spans thousands of leagues from its frozen northern peaks to the scorched plains of the south. Crossing the continent from one end to the other can take months by horse or airship, and even the most detailed maps leave great swaths of land unmarked. Its sheer scale gives rise to distinct climates, ecosystems, and people, each molded by the [[Aether]] currents that flow beneath the surface.

The north is cool and temperate, marked by the granite teeth of the [[Shardspine Range]] and the subterranean halls of the [[Ember Forgeholds]]. Snowmelt rivers feed the fertile lowlands of the [[Silver Crown]], whose rolling hills and golden plains form the heart of human civilization.

Moving Southward, the forests thicken and the air grows warm and humid. Here the [[Greenwood Pact]] holds dominion - a realm of colossal trees, spirit-haunted groves, and primal harmony where the land itself seems alive. Beyond the tree line, the world fractures into the volcanic scars and twisted terrains of the [[Abyssal Brood]], where the world steams and the horizon glows with unending fire.

To the east, the continent opens into the mystic expanses of the Azure Enclave and the sprawling territories of the [[Gynareth Dominion]] - enchanted forests, ancient riverlands, sky-cliffs, and silk-web woods where [[Aether]] runs strong. Along the southern edge between the Dominion and the Brood, the [[Crimson Court]] wedges like a burning scar, its borders shifting with war and pact alike.

At the center stands [[Veythall]], the Neutral City - the axis of trade, politics, and ambition. From tis stone roads and sky-piercing spires, routes radiate to every nation and frontier.

Beyond every coast lies the [[Endless Sea]], cradle and boundary of known life, its tempests hiding ruins of lost ages and whispering of lands yet unseen.

Though each realm claims its borders, Eryndor is never still - rivers shift, forests reclaim, volcanoes awaken, and wars redraw maps faster than any cartographer can ink them.
