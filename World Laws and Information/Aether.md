Aether is the invisible current that sustains all existence within [[Eryndor]]. It is not simply *magic* but the animating essence of creation, a radiant energy that flows through the land like blood. Every creature, plant, stone, and gust of wind holds a trace of it. The balance of these traces determines whether a region thrives or decays.

## Origin of Aether
Scholars and sages agree that Aether predates all mortal life, yet none can prove its true genesis. The [[Elves|Elven]] seers believe Aether is the breath of the [[World Mother]], exhaled when the first forests grew. The [[Dwarves]] claim it erupted from the molten heart of [[Eryndor]] when the Shardspine Mountains were forged. The Demons whisper that it was stolen from a dead god whose body became the world itself. Most agree on one truth: Aether is finite in concentration but eternal in cycle - its destructions always birthing renewal elsewhere. The balance of this primordial flow is the invisible law binding heaven, earth, and soul.
## Nature and Flow
Aether moves in vast subterranean rivers known as [[Ley-Lines]], rising to the surface where it gathers in [[Aether Wells]] - springs of condensed power that shimmer in every color of the spectrum. These well pulse in rhythm with the moons, the tides, and even the collective emotions of living beings. When undisturbed Aether cycles cleanly between the earth, the sky, and the souls of the living, renewing life with each pass.

## Interactions with Life
Every soul in [[Eryndor]] acts as a vessel for Aether. Mages learn to refine their internal flow, warriors channel it through strength and instinct, artisans weave it into song, steel, and prayer. When a being dies, its residual Aether seeps back into the world, joining the cycle once more. Overuse however, tears at this rhythm. Draining color from the land and drawing the first whispers of [[corruption]].

## Forms and Qualities
Aether manifests in multiple states:
* **Raw Aether:** Unrefined, radiant, and dangerously unstable.
* **Refined Aether:** Contained within crystals, runes, or forges. Used for enchantments and Aether-driven machines.
* **Living Aether:** Found within souls, beasts, plants, monsters and all other forms of life. It is self sustaining and reactive to emotion
* **Wild Aether:** Unbound energy released by storms, eclipses, or ley ruptures, capable of spontaneous creation or destruction.

## Philosophies of Use
* The [[Silver Crown]] treats it as divine providence, wielded by sanctioned knights and clerics
* The [[Ember Forgeholds]] see it as fuel to be mined and mastered, forging [[Aether-Steel]] and [[Rune Engines]]
* The [[Azur Enclave]] views it as sacred harmony - something to be guided not owned.
* The [[Greenwood Pact]] communes with it instinctively, drawing strength from natures living flow.
* The [[Crimson Court]] corrupts and twists it, binding Aether into contracts of flesh and soul.
* The [[Gynareth Dominion]] channels it through instinct and inheritance, shaping their monstrous vitality.
* The [[Abyssal Brood]] simply consume it, devouring its purity to birth abominations and increase their power.

## Consequences of Imbalance
When Aether is hoarded, bled, or weaponized beyond natures limit, it destabilizes the cycle. Plants wither, skies darken, and living things mutate. Such zones - called [[Aether Scars]] - are the birthplace of nightmares and the heartlands of the [[Abyssal Brood|Brood]]. Conversely, purified regions called [[Aether Sanctums]] radiate life and renewal, often guarded by ancient spirits or sacred orders.

## Applications of Aether
Through centuries of study and experimentation, every faction has found ways to harness Aether for both craft and conquest.
* **[[Spell Craft]]**: The manipulation of Aether through incantation, gesture, and will. Each school of magic - fire, water, earth, wind, and beyond - draws from a unique resonance within the Aether spectrum.
* [[Aether-Forging]]: Dwarven and human smiths use controlled ley heat and rune matrices to forge [[Aether-Steel]], a metal that hums faintly when near strong magic.
* [[Aether-Tech]]: In advanced cities, Aether-powered lifts, lamps, and weaponry blur the line between arcane and mechanical
* [[Runecraft]]: The Art of embedding refined Aether crystals into armor, weapons, or architecture to grant lasting enchantments.
* [[Spiritual Channeling]]: Priests and druid draw ambient Aether through faith or emotion, shaping miracles without formal training.
* [[Soul Binding]]: A forbidden practice of capturing or redirecting a beings life-essence to empower objects, soldiers, or undead constructs.
