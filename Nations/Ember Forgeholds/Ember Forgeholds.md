Deep beneath the mountains burn the forges of the Ember Forgeholds, dwarven citadels that have thrived since the **First Songs**. Known as **Oath-Keepers** and master smiths, the Forgeholds produce unmatched weapons, armor, and war machines. Their society prizes honor, contracts, and innovation, though their greed and grudges are legendary. To outsiders, the dwarves and both stern allies and dangerous creditors. Forgehold relics often find their way into adventurer hands, for a steep price.
