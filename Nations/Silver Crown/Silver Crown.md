The Silver Crown is the largest human monarchy in  Eryndor, ruled by dynasties who claim divine right. Their lands stretch across fertile plans, fortified cities, and farmlands that feed half the continent. Armies of knights and soldiers march under banners of gold and crimson, sworn to protect the Crown's borders and honor. Nobles vie for power through marriage, intrigue, and debt, while knightly orders prove their worth through tournaments and crusades.

The current sovereign is King Alaric III, a charismatic yet ruthless ruler whose ambition is to unite all human realms beneath his banner. His daughter, Princess Seraphina Valenhardt, is a cunning and elegant schemer who conceals sharp political instincts beneath her graceful poise. 

Politically, the Silver Crown maintains fragile alliances with the [[Azur Enclave]] and open trade with the [[Ember Forgeholds]]. They are bitter rivals with the [[Greenwood Pact]], locked in border skirmishes and territorial disputes, and consider the [[Abyssal Brood]] abominations a plight against the world and a cancer that must be eradicated. They keep an uneasy watch on the [[Crimson Court]], whose temptation's and bargains threaten human souls, while the [[Gynareth Dominion]] is feared as an existential threat for their predatory nature and open hunts of man.

Life in the Crown lands is stable, ordered, and disciplined, but politics are treacherous.
