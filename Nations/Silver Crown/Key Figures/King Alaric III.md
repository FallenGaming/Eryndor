The ambitious and iron-willed monarch of the Silver Crown, claiming divine right over all of humanity.

### BASIC INFORMATION
Name: Alaric Valenhardt
Race: Human
Sex: Male
Age: 46
Height: 6'0"

### PERSONALITY
Charismatic and commanding, yet ruthless beneath the surface. See's himself as a unifier and chosen vessel of the gods. He wont hesitate to utilize fear and cunning in place of diplomacy if required. Balances grandeur in public with cold pragmatism in private.

### APPEARANCE
Broad framed, slightly weathered but handsome features. Hair short and golden, streaked with early gray. Green eyes sharp with calculation. Despite spending much of his time locked in royal paperwork, his body is well trained and muscular from his life spent training with the royal knights.

### CLOTHING PREFERENCE
Prefers regal white-and-gold robes layered over gilded ceremonial armor. Wears the "Crown of Dawn", a relic symbolizing his divine mandate.
